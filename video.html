Real News Platform

The Real News Platform seeks to tackle the issue of government censorship by creating an international censorship watchdog community. The platform will allow its users to share news articles and other relevant information about events that are being distorted or outright denied by the governments of various countries who seek to withhold information from their citizens.

This video shows a specific use case in which articles and information about the spread of the Covid 19 (corona) virus could be posted by the international community. During the spread of the virus the Chinese government was criticized for using state-run media outlets to publish innacurately low figures in order to maintain an illusion of control, leaving many citizen without the means to protect themselves from the virus. Here we see how this platform could serve these citizens by allowing them access to current and correct informtion.
